The Boeing Business Jet (BBJ) is the first plane which was created for the business aviation by the Boeing Company. The plane was especially constructed for the representatives of the world's elite and for the corporate customers. This model has already been enjoyed by businessmen and private owners for 10 years. The next modifications of the BBJ – BBJ2 and BBJ3 – have got differences in cabin sizes and ranges of flights.

The flight range of the plane is 11519 km, it allows to make direct flights from London to Johannesburg, from Paris to Los-Angeles, from Moscow to Vladivostok.The BBJ is certified for outbound flights over extensive water spaces. Also it is equipped by the satellite navigation system and by the perspective complexes of aeronautical equipments. The BBJ airliners demonstrate 99,9 % the accuracy of keeping to schedule. It is an one of the best index inside the aviation industry.

The plane provides the maximum maintenance flexibility. Configuration of the cabin could be changed according to current demands by owners. There is comfortable environment as for working as relaxing or just having a good time on the plane board.

The plane interior is made for each customer individually. Not only a spacious living room with massive furniture or business office with a grand work desk but even bedroom with a king-size bed, a capacious luggage space, a comfortable functional kitchen and a separate bathroom equipped with full-size shower inside – all this can be placed on the plane board. The satellite communication system which is available on the board allows to the passengers to keep up their business contacts whenever the plane is being located.

The Boeing Business Jet 2 (BBJ 2) is modern business airliner kind of BBJ. The new model has got spacier cabin, the volume is increased by 25% compared with BBJ and it is 93,27 sq.m. The developers have created adjustable arrangement of the cabin for passengers which can make different variants of capacity from 8-9 to 50 seats on account of fast shifting of the modular basic pieces of equipment.

Flying range of BBJ2 is fewer than such point of its predecessor for 1000 km. However, it is quit enough to make transatlantic flights. The business airliner is equipped by efficient jet engines CFM56-7 which are produced by the CFM International Company. At the request of the customer the plane could be provided with a set of office equipment, high-speed Internet access, fax connection and satellite television.

The BBJ2 cabin is the spaciest cabin among cabins of all other business-airliners. It allows to place on the board a mobile office with all necessary equipments or luxurious apartments. For private owners an option of arrangement is suggested including bedroom with a king-size bed, bathroom, an individual apartments for VIP-persons and a place for their entourage. For corporate transportation there are spacier arrangements with comfortable folding chairs.

Interior of the BBJ2 cabin is formed according to individual project for each customer. The Boeing Company suggests for their customers an unique instrument for making interiors for their plains – it is the SKYHOME program. By means of this program owner of the BBJ2 can arrange the cabin space visually at own discretion.

The Boeing Business Jet 3 (BBJ 3) The Boeing Business Jet 3 (BBJ 3) is created on the base of passenger's model of the Boeing 737-900ER with range of flight increased. The BBJ3 can conduct long-distance flights from Moscow to Siattle or from Moscow to Singapore.

The BBJ3 which has got the cabin length of 32,66 m and width of 3,35 m is considered as the most comfortable among the planes in its class. A space of the cabin is 104 sq.m, it is 35% more than the BBJ2 cabin. Spacious cabin allows to create luxurious interior and provides passengers with the highest level of comfort.

A space of the luggage compartment is extended on 89% compared with the first modification. BBJ3 as all other Boeing business jets is equipped with high-level beveled winglets what allows to increase the range of flight while keeping of fuel consumption level and load indicators.

The Leavitt-Weaver Company which specializes in the furniture and interiors design is developing interiors for BBJ3. Designers examine closely the lifestyle and habits of the persons who are the potential customers of the business jets this level, and they develop fundamentally new configuration and furnishing of the BBJ cabin.
